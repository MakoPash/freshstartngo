About Us

In Zimbabwe, where mental health struggles often remain hidden behind silence and stigma, the FreshStart Mental Health Support Organisation rises as a bold and compassionate force for change. More than an initiative, FreshStart is a movement, one that seeks to restore hope, dignity, and emotional resilience to communities across the Harare Metropolitan Province. It listens to the unheard, reaches the unseen, and stands with those who feel forgotten. By blending data-driven strategy with deep grassroots engagement, FreshStart is reshaping how mental wellness is understood, supported, and sustained.

FreshStart Mental Health Support Organisation is a dynamic mental health initiative committed to building emotionally resilient communities through inclusive support, strategic advocacy, and local empowerment. It confronts stigma head-on, expands access to care, and strengthens the networks that hold communities together. At its heart, FreshStart believes that healing is not just clinical, it’s communal, cultural, and deeply human.

Its strategy is anchored in seven key pillars:

  • Listening to unheard voices through baseline surveys that uncover mental health needs and service gaps
  • Launching targeted advocacy campaigns to challenge stigma and shift public perceptions
  • Mapping institutions and forging strategic alliances to build a united front for mental wellness
  • Hosting culturally sensitive wellness workshops that speak to lived experiences
  • Delivering accessible counselling and support services that meet people where they are
  • Equipping everyday heroes—educators, caregivers, and peer counsellors—with tools to heal and uplift their communities
  • Implementing robust monitoring and evaluation systems to ensure accountability, learning, and lasting impact

Together, these efforts form a holistic framework for sustainable mental health transformation in Zimbabwe.
FreshStart isn’t just building programs, it’s building a future where mental wellness is a right, not a privilege.

Our Mission

To become a beacon of hope and we are dedicated to promoting a drug-free society. We guide each person, in a dignified manner, to the transformation of self-discovery and healing.

Our Vision

We aim to become Zimbabwe's preferred centre for mental health, healing and self-discovery. Driven by empathy and dedication, we envision to build resilient people and communities where there is no drug and substance abuse.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Empathy & Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy, kindness, and understanding.

Diversity & Inclusivity

We embrace and celebrate the diversity of all individuals, ensuring that everyone feels welcome and valued in our programs and services.

Tolerance

We embrace and respect all individuals, regardless of their differences, fostering an environment of mutual understanding and acceptance.

Teamwork & Transparency

We believe in the power of collaboration and open communication to achieve our shared goals.

Making a Difference

Since our inception, FreshStart has touched the lives of thousands across Zimbabwe. From providing crisis intervention to facilitating long-term therapy, from educating communities to training healthcare workers, we're proud to be part of the mental health revolution in our country.
