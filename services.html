Our Services

Comprehensive mental health support tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals and communities across Zimbabwe.

Professional Psychosocial Support

Access to qualified mental health professionals providing individual and family psychosocial support services tailored to your unique needs.

  • Licensed therapists
  • Individual & family sessions
  • Culturally sensitive care
  • Referral services

Community Support Groups

Regular peer-led support groups where individuals can share experiences, learn coping strategies, and build meaningful connections in a safe environment.

  • Weekly group sessions
  • Peer-to-peer support
  • Confidential and safe space
  • Available in multiple locations

24/7 Crisis Hotline

Immediate support for those experiencing a mental health emergency.

  • Trained crisis counselors
  • Immediate intervention
  • Suicide prevention
  • Emergency referrals

Mental Health Education

Comprehensive workshops and seminars designed to increase mental health literacy and reduce stigma in communities.

  • Community workshops
  • School programs
  • Workplace seminars
  • Educational materials

Peer Support

Trained peer supporters and Mental Wellness Champions who understand the journey to mental wellness.

  • Identification & Certification of Champions
  • Co-creation of Solutions
  • Peer Related Recovery Pathways
  • Rehabilitation and Recovery Support

Youth Mental Health Program

Specialized programs for young people addressing the unique mental health challenges they face.

  • Age-appropriate (10-24 years) support
  • Institution-based partnerships
  • Youth peer learning and support
  • Positive parental engagement
  • Inter-generational linkages and support
