Who We Are

Our Story

FreshStart is a community-driven initiative dedicated to transforming the landscape of mental wellness in Zimbabwe. We are committed to dismantling stigma through compassionate care, community-led support, and professional advocacy that restores dignity to those we serve.

Integrity

We operate under the highest standards of ethics and safeguarding to ensure a secure, professional environment for our community while adhering to international best practices.

Our Vision

To become Zimbabwe's leading center for excellence in mental health support and comprehensive substance abuse rehabilitation.

The Mission

To serve as a beacon of hope for a drug-free society. We believe that true healing begins with community care, fostering resilience and recovery in every heart we touch.

Our Focus

Counselling

Compassionate, professional support for those navigating substance abuse recovery and mental health challenges.

Workshops

Engaging educational sessions designed to build mental health awareness and community resilience.

Training

Equipping caregivers and educators with the essential tools to identify and support mental health needs.

Advocacy

Strategic campaigns aimed at challenging stigma and promoting mental wellness at a national level.
